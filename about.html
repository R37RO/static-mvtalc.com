About Maa Vaishnavi TALC Industries

From Himalayan foothills to global markets - A journey of excellence, innovation, and unwavering commitment to quality

Our Heritage

A Journey of Excellence

Almost two decades back we started the MINING operation at 'Beesa Nakuri Bageshwar', foothills of Himalaya region and established a FACTORY at Haldwani, Uttarakhand for processing soapstone lumps to produce TALC POWDER. What began as a vision to harness the pristine mineral wealth of the Himalayas has evolved into a global enterprise serving diverse industries with uncompromising quality and reliability.
