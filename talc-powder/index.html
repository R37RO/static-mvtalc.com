about talc powder

Talc is the world’s softest and most hydrophobic mineral. It adds new features to products that use it as a raw material and makes processes more efficient. It’s salient features help it act as the most effective filler material. While it is already used in various applications, constant research in the field of its usage is unraveling its wider usage and advantages.

Talc Products

Talc production Stages

White Powder | The Product

Our factories are certified pursuant to quality management standard. We supply only top quality products - so ensuring consistency and quality is a major part of our corporate culture.

stage 1   |   Mining Talc lumps

Raw talc is procured from approved source and Government certified mines which meets our specification. Great care is taken during the mining process to avoid contaminating the talc with other rock materials.

stage 2   |   Sorting talc lumps

Sorting the different talc ores according to their talc content and brightness is a key phase of the production process. Impurities are sometimes removed by mechanical processing. The ores are then dried, crushed and milled.

stage 3   |   Milling talc lumps

Talc milling technology, a complex process where the methods used depend both on the ore type and the final application.

stage 4   |   Packaging talc powder

For the food and pharmaceuticals industries, talc is packed in clean room provided with filtration system to avoid any other contamination and sterilized as per the requirement.

stage 5   |  Talc powder supply

For the industries like food, paper, cosmetics etc Talc powder(Mg3SiO4O10(OH)2). We are regular supplier to Birla Organization Centuray Pulp & paper Lalkuan, Prolific Papers Pvt Ltd, Kashipur, Uttarakhand, Flexituff Ventures International Limited Uttarakhand, Katyayani Paper Mills Pvt.Ltd Kashipur Nicer paper mills jammu & Kashmir. & several cosmetic industries and polymer industries and paint industries etc.
