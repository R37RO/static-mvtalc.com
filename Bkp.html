About Talc

World's Softest & Most Hydrophobic Mineral

Talc is the world's softest and most hydrophobic mineral, with a hardness of 1 on the Mohs scale. Its unique lamellar structure and exceptional properties make it an invaluable additive across multiple industries. Our premium Himalayan talc exhibits superior brightness, fineness, and purity, making it the preferred choice for demanding applications worldwide.
