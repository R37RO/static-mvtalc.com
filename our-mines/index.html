OUR MINES

Vaishnavi Mines | Shri Vijay Bhawani Corporation | Maa Vaishnavi Enterprise

Maa Vaishnavi Talc Industries, the leading global supplier of best quality Talc from our own
mines located at Reema, Bageshwar, foothills of Himalaya region in a vast area from which approx.
18,000 M.T. of soap stone lumps is mined annually.

Almost two decades back we started the mining operation at Bageshwar, foothills of Himalaya
region and established a factory at Haldwani, Uttarakhand for processing soapstone lumps to
produce talc powder.

Since then we are having several mines of high-quality talc at Bageshwar and Pithoragarh District
of Uttarakhand State, Today we are supplying soapstone lumps and powder to most talc-based industries as a raw material and filler, this strength of Maa Vaishnavi Talc Industries is establishing as a growing and innovative enterprise.

We also develop talc grades tailored for maximum performance along with logistics support to any
part of the world.

We have built processing capabilities that can deliver the final product (Talc) with brightness
ranging from 80% to 98% and fineness from 300 mesh to 700+ mesh. Having hand full of
experience in handling this product, we can cater to most of its applications, to various industries.
