ABOUT US

Almost two decades back we started the MINING 

operation at “Beesa Nakuri Bageshwar”, foothills of Himalaya region and established a FACTORY at Haldwani, 

Uttarakhand for processing soapstone lumps to produce TALC POWDER

Maa Vaishnavi TALC Industries, the leading global supplier of best quality Talc from our own mines located at Reema, Bageshwar, foothills of Himalaya region in a vast area from which approx.18,000 M.T. of soap stone lumps is mined annually. We have built processing and Transportation ‘Maa Vaishnavi Transport‘ capabilities as that can deliver the final product (TALC) with brightness ranging from 80% to 98% and fineness from 300 mesh to 700+ mesh. 

Our Mission

To extract, manufacture, process and supply premium quality minerals and talc in India and abroad using ultra-modern technology, progressive Research & Development strategies, dexterous manpower and flawless Logistics to serve our privileged clients pertaining to diverse industry types.

Our Vision

To build ceaseless relationships with our clients, vendors and associates banked on trust, reliability, and punctuality for timely delivering pre-eminent raw minerals and talc around the world benefitting a vast range of industries and their consumers.
